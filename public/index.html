Core Systems

Everything you need to run a competitive airline from the ground up

✈
Fleet Management
Purchase, lease, and maintain aircraft. Manage A/B/C/D checks, assign registrations, and optimise fleet utilisation across your route network.
🌐
Route Network
Build routes across real-world airports with realistic ATC waypoint routing via airways, SIDs, STARs, and North Atlantic Tracks.
💰
Financial Management
Track weekly revenue, costs, and profit. Detailed breakdowns for fuel, crew, maintenance, airport fees, and staff salaries.
👥
Staff & Operations
Manage departments including pilots, cabin crew, engineering, and ground services. Set salary levels and outsource contractors.
🏢
Banking & Loans
Apply for loans from multiple banks with dynamic interest rates based on your airline's credit score, reputation, and profitability.
📈
Competition
Compete against real players and AI airlines. Track competitor routes, pricing, and performance. Climb the revenue leaderboard.
