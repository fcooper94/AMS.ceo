Getting Started

After logging in with your VATSIM account, you'll be taken to the World Selection screen. Choose a world to join, then set up your airline by selecting a name, ICAO/IATA codes, region, and base airport. Your airline starts with $1,000,000 to purchase or lease your first aircraft.

Purchasing: You own the aircraft outright. Higher upfront cost but no monthly payments. You can sell the aircraft later.

Leasing: Lower upfront cost with monthly lease payments. Great for expanding quickly, but you don't own the aircraft and must return it when the lease ends.

Operations

Create routes between airports, then schedule flights on those routes. You can set up recurring schedules (daily, weekly) or one-time flights. The system automatically manages aircraft turnaround times and crew scheduling.

Aircraft require regular maintenance checks to remain airworthy:

  • Daily Check: Quick inspection, ~1 hour, every 1-2 days
  • Weekly Check: More thorough, ~2-3 hours, every 7-8 days
  • A Check: Every 800-1000 flight hours, ~6-12 hours
  • C Check: Every 2 years, 2-4 weeks in hangar
  • D Check: Every 5-7 years, 2-3 months (major overhaul)

You can enable auto-scheduling for maintenance to avoid manual scheduling.

World & Time

Each world has accelerated time (typically 60x real time). This means 1 real minute = 1 game hour. The world continues to progress even when you're not playing, so your scheduled flights will operate and your airline will earn/spend money.

Account & Credits

Credits are used to maintain your active participation in worlds. 1 credit is consumed per game week of play. New users start with a generous amount of credits. If credits run low, you have a grace period of 1 game month (-4 credits) before your world participation is paused.
