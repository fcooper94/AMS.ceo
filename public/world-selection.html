Outsourced Ground Handling

Your airline doesn't yet have the ground staff or equipment for baggage handling, boarding, and fuelling. You'll need a ground handling partner at every airport you operate from. Choose a contractor that fits your priorities — speed, cost, or passenger experience.

Outsourced Engineering & MRO

Without your own hangars or engineering team, all maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) work must be contracted out. Your choice of engineering partner directly affects aircraft reliability and operating costs. As your fleet grows, you may invest in your own MRO facilities.

DECLARE BANKRUPTCY

WARNING: This action cannot be undone.

Declaring bankruptcy will:

• Remove your airline from this world

• Delete all your assets and progress

• Free up your airline code for others

You can rejoin this world later with a fresh start.

END WORLD

WARNING: This action cannot be undone.

Ending this world will:

• Permanently stop all game time progression

• Freeze all AI airline operations

• Mark the world as completed

You can still view the world after ending it.
